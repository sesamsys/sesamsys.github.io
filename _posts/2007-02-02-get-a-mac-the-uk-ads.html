As far as I know until recently only the Japanese Apple subsite had separate 'Get a Mac' ads: Macをはじめよう

But apparently there are now UK versions of the known Mac ads, which proved to be far more hilarious than the originals. British accent, British vocabulary, all the fun. Just compare "Work vs. Home" (Apple - Get a Mac - Watch The TV Ads) with "Pie Chart" (Apple (UK and Ireland) - Get a Mac - Watch The New Ads)

"For example this light gray area could represent shenanigans and tomfoolery. While this dark gray area could represent hijinks. And you see here we further divided hijinks into capers, monkey business and just larking about..."

Instead of hangout time and just kicking it.

Priceless.
